## 2. The Platform Service Fee — Complete Formula

### 2.1 Standard Formula

A **Platform Service Fee** is applied to every transaction processed through the Platform. The fee is calculated as follows:

> **Platform Service Fee = $0.50 (flat fee) + 10% of the Base Transaction Amount**

Where:
- **$0.50 flat fee** is a fixed charge applied to every transaction regardless of amount;
- **10% of the Base Transaction Amount** is a variable percentage assessed on the pre-fee purchase price.

### 2.2 PayPal Surcharge

When the buyer selects PayPal as their payment method, an additional surcharge of **$0.50** is applied:

> **Platform Service Fee (PayPal) = $0.50 (flat fee) + 10% of the Base Transaction Amount + $0.50 (PayPal surcharge)**

This surcharge reflects the higher transaction processing costs imposed by PayPal relative to card-based payment methods. The PayPal surcharge is disclosed at checkout before payment confirmation.

### 2.3 Complete Fee Formula Summary

| Payment Method | Formula | Components |
|---|---|---|
| Card (Stripe) | $0.50 + (Base × 10%) | Flat fee + percentage |
| PayPal | $0.50 + (Base × 10%) + $0.50 | Flat fee + percentage + PayPal surcharge |

## 4. Fee Naming Conventions by Context

The Platform Service Fee is applied across multiple transaction types. While the underlying formula is identical across all transaction types, the fee may appear under context-specific names to accurately reflect the nature of the transaction:

| Fee Label | Context of Use | Formula Applied |
|---|---|---|
| **Service Fee** | Support Plan subscription billing (yearly and monthly) | $0.50 + 10% of subscription price |
| **Marketplace Fee** | Extension and theme purchases on the Bazarix Marketplace | $0.50 + 10% of listing price |
| **Administrative Fee** | One-time setup or onboarding charges | $0.50 + 10% of setup charge |
| **Technology Fee** | API access tier charges | $0.50 + 10% of access fee |

Regardless of the label displayed, all fees follow the formula set forth in Section 2 of this disclosure. The Platform will always display the specific label applicable to your transaction.

## 5. Justification for the Platform Service Fee

The Platform Service Fee covers the following operational costs:

- **Secure global payment infrastructure:** Payment gateway processing, SSL/TLS certificate costs, PCI-DSS compliance infrastructure, and fraud detection services;
- **International disbursement logistics:** Costs associated with routing payments to sellers via Stripe Connect across international jurisdictions, currency conversion overhead, and payout batching;
- **Currency conversion operations:** Exchange rate risk management and conversion costs where applicable to cross-border transactions;
- **Platform maintenance:** Server infrastructure, database operations, API maintenance, security monitoring, and business continuity costs.

As required by the Terms of Service: "A Platform Service Fee is applied to all transactions to cover secure global payment infrastructure, international disbursement logistics, currency conversion operations, and platform maintenance."

ReadyPixels confirms that no fee component exceeds the actual operational costs associated with processing transactions through the Platform, consistent with CFPB guidance on fee reasonableness.

## 7. Fees in the Marketplace Revenue Split

For Marketplace transactions, the fee structure interacts with the seller revenue split as follows:

**Buyer pays:**
- Listing Price (the amount set by the seller)
- Platform Service Fee ($0.50 + 10% of Listing Price)
- PayPal Surcharge (+ $0.50 if paying by PayPal)

**Of the Listing Price:**
- Seller receives: **70%** (disbursed via Stripe Connect)
- Platform receives: **30%** (Platform Commission)

**Platform also retains:** 100% of the Platform Service Fee collected from the buyer.

**Example — $50.00 listing, card payment:**

| Party | Amount |
|---|---|
| Buyer pays total | $55.50 |
| Seller receives | $35.00 (70% of $50.00) |
| Platform Commission | $15.00 (30% of $50.00) |
| Platform Service Fee | $5.50 |
| Platform total received | $20.50 |

## 8. Subscription Fee Calculation — Support Plans

For Support Plan subscriptions, the Platform Service Fee is assessed on the subscription price at the time of each billing event (initial purchase and each renewal).

**Monthly Plan Pricing Note:** The Monthly Support Plan is priced at (Yearly Price ÷ 12) × 1.25. The Platform Service Fee is then applied to this monthly price as a separate fee. The 25% monthly uplift and the Platform Service Fee are distinct charges that serve different purposes.

**Example — Yearly plan at $120.00:**

| | Yearly Plan | Monthly Plan |
|---|---|---|
| Base subscription price | $120.00/year | $12.50/month |
| Platform Service Fee | $0.50 + $12.00 = $12.50 | $0.50 + $1.25 = $1.75 |
| Total per period | $132.50 | $14.25 |
