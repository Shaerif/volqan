## 2. Support Plan Refunds

### 2.1 Yearly Support Plans — Prorated Refund Window

**Yearly Support Plan subscribers are eligible for a prorated refund within fourteen (14) calendar days of the payment date**, subject to the conditions set forth in this Section 2.

**Prorated Refund Calculation:**

> **Refund Amount = (Yearly Plan Price × (Days Remaining / 365)) — Non-Refundable Fees**

"Days Remaining" means the number of full calendar days remaining in the subscription term at the time the refund request is received by ReadyPixels, calculated from the date of receipt of the refund request to the scheduled renewal date.

**Example:** A subscriber purchases a Yearly Support Plan at $120.00 on April 5, 2026. They submit a valid refund request on April 15, 2026 (10 days after purchase, 355 days remaining in the term). The prorated refund would be calculated as ($120.00 × 355/365) = approximately $116.71, minus any non-refundable Platform Service Fees paid.

### 2.2 Yearly Plan — Refund Eligibility Conditions

To be eligible for a prorated refund on a Yearly Support Plan, you must:

(a) Submit a refund request within fourteen (14) calendar days of the original payment date;

(b) Contact ReadyPixels at sharif@readypixels.com with the subject line "Refund Request — Yearly Plan" and provide your account email, Installation ID, and the reason for the refund request;

(c) Not have initiated a payment dispute or chargeback with your financial institution or payment processor with respect to the payment in question — doing so will render you ineligible for a refund under this policy.

### 2.3 Yearly Plan — Outside Refund Window

Refund requests for Yearly Support Plans received more than fourteen (14) calendar days after the original payment date will be reviewed on a **case-by-case basis** at ReadyPixels' sole discretion. No refund is guaranteed outside the fourteen-day window. ReadyPixels may, at its sole discretion, offer a prorated account credit applicable to future purchases rather than a monetary refund.

### 2.4 Monthly Support Plans — Non-Refundable

**Monthly Support Plan payments are non-refundable in all circumstances once the billing date has passed.** By subscribing to a Monthly Support Plan, you acknowledge that:

(a) The monthly fee is charged on your billing date and covers the full monthly billing period;

(b) No prorated or partial refunds are available for unused days within a monthly billing period;

(c) Canceling your Monthly Support Plan will prevent future charges but will not result in a refund of the current period's payment;

(d) Your access to Support Plan benefits, including the right to suppress the Attribution Notice, continues until the end of the paid billing period, after which it terminates.

This non-refundable policy for monthly plans reflects the 25% monthly pricing uplift already built into the Monthly plan, which partially compensates for the administrative and financial overhead of monthly billing cycles.

### 2.5 Automatic Renewal Refunds

If you were charged for an automatic subscription renewal and did not receive advance notice of the renewal as required by applicable law, please contact us at sharif@readypixels.com within seven (7) days of the charge. ReadyPixels will review such requests and, where required by applicable law, process a refund for the unauthorized renewal charge.

## 3. Marketplace Purchase Refunds

### 3.1 General Non-Refundable Policy

Extensions, themes, templates, and other digital products purchased through the Bazarix Marketplace are **non-refundable after the download or license key delivery has occurred**, unless the listing is materially misrepresented as described in Section 3.2.

Digital products are delivered immediately upon payment confirmation. Because the buyer receives access to the product at the moment of purchase, refunds are not routinely available.

### 3.2 Material Misrepresentation Exception

You may be eligible for a refund of a Marketplace purchase if you can demonstrate that the purchased product is materially misrepresented — meaning the product fundamentally fails to perform the core functionality described in the listing and the seller is unable to remedy the defect within a reasonable time.

To request a misrepresentation refund:

(a) Contact ReadyPixels at sharif@readypixels.com within seven (7) calendar days of purchase;

(b) Provide a detailed description of the defect, evidence that the product fails to perform as described, and documentation of any communications with the seller;

(c) Allow ReadyPixels up to ten (10) business days to investigate and make a determination.

ReadyPixels' determination in misrepresentation cases is final.

### 3.3 Marketplace Refund Mechanics

If a Marketplace refund is approved:

(a) The buyer's license key is immediately revoked and access to downloads is terminated;

(b) The seller's corresponding earnings for the transaction are clawed back or deducted from future payouts;

(c) ReadyPixels retains its 30% Platform Commission on the transaction and the Platform Service Fee (both non-refundable per Section 4).

## 5. Effect of Refunds on Attribution Removal Entitlement

### 5.1 Immediate Revocation Upon Refund

**Any approved refund of a Support Plan payment — whether partial, prorated, or full — immediately and automatically revokes the subscriber's entitlement to suppress the Attribution Notice.**

Upon the processing of a refund:

(a) The License API will reflect the revocation of the attribution removal entitlement immediately upon completion of the refund;

(b) The subscriber's Volqan installation will restore the Attribution Notice on its next license check (within 24 hours, or at next server restart);

(c) Continued suppression of the Attribution Notice after revocation constitutes an unauthorized removal and a material breach of the Open Core Attribution License v1.0 and the Terms of Service, with all consequences described therein.

### 5.2 No Grace Period

There is no grace period for restoration of the Attribution Notice following a refund. The obligation to display the Attribution Notice applies from the moment the refund is processed.

### 5.3 Partial Refunds and Attribution

Where a prorated partial refund is issued for a Yearly Support Plan (Section 2.1), the subscriber loses the entitlement to suppress the Attribution Notice for the entire remainder of the original subscription term, not just the refunded portion. A subscriber who receives a partial refund and wishes to continue suppressing the Attribution Notice must purchase a new active Support Plan.
